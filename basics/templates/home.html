Traditional System
  • Manual Check-Ins: Check-in is on paper and pen-based logbooks.
  • Non-confidential: Visitor entry system becomes a public document that any guest can seek through.
  • No Identity Verification: A manual process cannot authenticate the visitor's mobile number.
  • Complex Process: Informing the host on the visitor's arrival.
  • No Offline Synchronization: They rely entirely on physical logbooks.
